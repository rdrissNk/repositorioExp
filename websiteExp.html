Eagles

Eagles are large, powerful birds of prey. They have keen eyesight and strong talons. Found on every continent except Antarctica, eagles are symbols of freedom and strength.

Penguins

Penguins are flightless birds adapted to life in the ocean. They are excellent swimmers and found primarily in the Southern Hemisphere, especially Antarctica.

Parrots

Parrots are colorful, intelligent birds known for their ability to mimic sounds and speech. They are social creatures and can live for many decades.

Owls

Owls are nocturnal birds of prey with large forward-facing eyes. They are silent fliers with excellent night vision and hearing.
